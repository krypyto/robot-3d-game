# 3D Room Robot Game

A thrilling 3D adventure game built with Unity, where you control a robot navigating through challenging rooms filled with obstacles, puzzles, and enemies.

## Description

Embark on an exciting journey as a robotic explorer in this immersive 3D game. Navigate through various rooms, solve puzzles, collect power-ups, and battle robotic foes in a futuristic environment.

## Features

- **Immersive 3D Environment**: Explore detailed rooms with realistic physics and lighting
- **Robot Customization**: Upgrade and customize your robot's abilities and appearance
- **Challenging Puzzles**: Solve complex puzzles to progress through levels
- **Combat System**: Engage in intense battles with enemy robots
- **Power-ups and Collectibles**: Gather items to enhance your robot's capabilities
- **Multiple Levels**: Experience diverse room designs and challenges

## Installation

### Prerequisites
- Unity 2021.3 or later
- Windows 10 or later (or compatible OS)

### Setup
1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open the project in Unity Hub
3. Ensure all required packages are installed (check Packages/manifest.json)
4. Open the main scene from Assets/Scenes/
5. Press Play to start the game

## Controls

- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**: Move the robot
- **Mouse**: Look around / Camera control
- **Space**: Jump
- **Left Click**: Primary attack
- **Right Click**: Secondary ability
- **E**: Interact with objects
- **Tab**: Open inventory/menu

## How to Play

1. **Objective**: Navigate through rooms, solve puzzles, and reach the exit
2. **Collect Power-ups**: Gather energy cells to upgrade your robot
3. **Combat**: Defeat enemy robots to clear paths
4. **Puzzles**: Use your robot's abilities to solve environmental puzzles
5. **Progression**: Complete levels to unlock new areas and challenges
